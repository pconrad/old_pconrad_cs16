Fast facts

  • The exam is:

    • Put your name on your notes sheet, and turn it in with your exam

Topics to emphasize (a partial list)

  • The way the stack is used to store local variables, and formal parameters of functions
  • The fact that the stack grows when functions are called, and shrinks when you return from a function
  • The different ways that parameters can be passed (see topics/paramPassing)
  • Array problems (as in lab07, and lab08)
  • Working with structs and pointers, and arrays of structs (as in lab06)
